Furnishing

Furnishing is a vital tool used for placing items that can be interacted with by personnel or is a requirement for specific links.

As of now, there are 9 available pieces of furniture that each have a specific function or reason. 
5 out of the 9 pieces of furniture have additional styles, adding up to a total of 14 different furniture.

Additional styles are denoted by the gear icon.

Some styles have additional features compared to their original counterpart.
